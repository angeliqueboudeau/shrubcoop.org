Become a member!

Online membership sign ups are coming soon! In the meantime, just pop in to the Zero Waste Hub and speak to one of our lovely volunteers to become a member.

As a member of SHRUB Coop you get to:

  • Swap preloved items in the Swapshop & get free stuff from the members section
  • Discounts at our events and workshops
  • Discounts on bicycle parts at the Wee Spoke Hub
  • Invitations to our members parties and Annual General Meeting
  • Take part in the cooperative’s decisions

To keep membership accessible for all, what you pay for membership is based on your income:

  • Income below £16k: £12 per year
  • Income between £16k - £27k: £18 per year
  • Income between £27k - £43k: £24 per year
  • Income above £43k: £30 per year

Full year memberships come with 5 complimentary Swapshop tokens in your account.
Half year memberships are available at 2/3rd of the annual price.
